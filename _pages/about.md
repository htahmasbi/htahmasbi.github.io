I am a **postdoctoral researcher** at the [Center for Advanced Systems Understanding (CASUS)](https://www.casus.science/)
at Helmholtz-Zentrum Dresden-Rossendorf (HZDR) in Görlitz, Germany.

My research focuses on **computational materials science**, **machine learning interatomic potentials**,
and **high-throughput calculations**. I develop and apply neural network potentials to study
structural and thermal properties of materials, bridging the gap between ab initio methods
and large-scale atomistic simulations.

I completed my **Ph.D. in Condensed-Matter Physics** at the Institute for Advanced Studies in Basic
Sciences (IASBS) in Zanjan, Iran, with a visiting research stay at **Leiden University** in the Netherlands.

Here you can find my [publications](/publications/), [talks](/talks/), and [CV](/cv/).
